You can consider managing your debts with the following options:

  • Pay off your debts as much as you can each month, at least pay the minimum payment you owe on each loan otherwise late and finance charges will be incurred.
  • However, if you pay only the minimum amount, you will pay more interest with longer repayment period. Check the comparison.
  • Pay off your debt with a higher repayment amount. Change repayment amount.
  • Pay off your debt earlier. Change repayment period.
  • If you can afford to pay more for some of the debts, pay the loan with the highest interest rate first.
  • Consolidate your debt.
