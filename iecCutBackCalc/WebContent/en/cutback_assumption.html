About cut-back calculator

Manage your spending regularly can help you grow your savings. This calculator helps you prioritise your needs and wants and look for non-essential expenses you can cut to save money. You can save the results on your computer, print out and do it later, or you can download the tool and use it offline.

It offers the following features which allow you to:

  1.   Download an Excel spreadsheet into your computer to work out the cut-back amount.
  2.   View and save your record in PDF format.
  3.   Print out your cut-back report.
  4.   Share the cut-back calculator to your friends by email.
  5.   Share the cut-back calculator to your friends via Facebook.
