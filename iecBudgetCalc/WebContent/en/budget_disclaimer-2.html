About budget planner

The budget planner is designed to help you understand your money habits and keep track of your finance.

It records all of your incomings (eg salary, pensions and benefits) and outgoings (including interest payments for mortgage repayment, other personal loans, cash advances, outstanding balance of credit card), and gives a breakdown of where your spending goes each week, month or year across the following broad categories: household, transport, food and drinks, leisure, shopping, health and beauty, education and profession, family and friends, taxation and financial commitments.

It offers the following features which allow you to:

  1.            Download an Excel spreadsheet into your computer to draw up a budget.
  2.            View and save your record in PDF format.
  3.            Print out your budget plan.
  4.            Share the budget planner to your friends by email.
  5.            Share the budget planner to your friends via Facebook.

The summary displays results on a monthly average basis, so you should choose the correct payment frequency for each item when entering your information. We use the following assumptions for our calculation:

1 year = 12 months = 52 weeks
1 month = 52 weeks / 12 = 4.33 weeks

The calculation result will be rounded to one decimal place where applicable.
